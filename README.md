# Monsterhall

Monsterhall is a fantasy guild management sim about running a guild hall beside a tower too lethal for ordinary adventurers to explore alone.

The game is about daily assignment choices: who works the hall, who rests, who supports expeditions, what to build, which contracts to accept, and how to keep growth ahead of debt and danger.

## Gameplay

- Hatch and grow a roster of monster companions.
- Assign companions to guild work, contracts, expeditions, or recovery.
- Earn gold, arcane residue, materials, eggs, and relics.
- Expand guild rooms, unlock species, and improve the surrounding outpost.
- Balance fatigue, stress, injury, instability, and debt milestones.
- Send teams into the tower for resources, discoveries, and new opportunities.

## Goal

Build the only guild capable of surviving the tower by proving that monster companions make impossible exploration practical.

## Controls

- Mouse: use the interface and make assignments.

## Current Scope

Playable management loop with hatching, assignments, daily results, expeditions, contracts, debt progression, rooms, species, and events.
