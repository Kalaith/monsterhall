**Game Design Document: Monsterhall**

**Version:** 1.0 (Concept Pitch)  
**Genre:** Cozy + Strategic Management Sim / Adult Monster Collector / Expedition Builder  
**Target Platform:** PC (Steam / Itch.io) – single-player, save-anytime  
**Tone:** Playful, lewd, systems-driven. Lighthearted corruption and harem fantasy with zero heavy narrative. Emphasis on “I built this place so my girls can fuck better and raid deeper.”  
**Art Style:** Minimalist top-down pixel town + portrait icons for monster girls. Low-poly or 2D sprite town that grows visibly around the central GuildJobs. All monster girls use static lewd portraits + trait icons (no full animations required).  
**Target Playtime:** 15–40 hours for full progression, endless post-game.

### 1. High Concept / Core Fantasy
You inherit a ruined keep built directly over an ancient, ever-shifting magical tower. In the depths lies a dormant Slime Spawner (later upgraded to a full Monster Girl Hatchery). Your job: turn the keep into a thriving, ever-expanding **monster girl brothel town** while sending your girls into the tower to harvest resources, eggs, relics, and lewd essences.

The town literally grows *around* the GuildJobs. Every new building you place improves either:
- Tower expeditions (deeper runs, better loot)
- GuildJobs performance (more clients, higher earnings, sluttier services)

Monster girls are both your **collectible harem** and your **dual-purpose workforce**:
- They raid the tower as a team.
- They work shifts in the GuildJobs to generate the gold and Lust Essence that fuel everything.

The fantasy is pure player-driven indulgence: raise, breed, mutate, and specialize lewd monster girls while watching your little ruined keep blossom into a glowing red-light district that services adventurers, nobles, and even tower monsters.

### 2. Core Gameplay Loop (The Heartbeat)
1. Hatch or breed new monster girls from the central Hatchery (starts as basic Slime Girls).
2. Assign girls to expeditions or GuildJobs shifts (or both on different days).
3. Send optimized teams into chosen tower floors with gear, buffs, and orders.
4. Girls return with:
   - Tower resources (stone, arcane shards, rare materials)
   - New eggs / mutations / lewd relics
   - Injuries, fatigue, or **pleasure corruption** (positive lewd trait)
   - Lust Essence (brothel-exclusive currency)
5. Spend everything to:
   - Expand and upgrade the GuildJobs (new rooms, kinks, client types)
   - Build town support buildings around it
   - Improve habitats, workshops, recovery spas, research labs
6. Stronger girls + better town → deeper tower runs → better rewards → repeat.

The loop is deliberately addictive because **the GuildJobs and the Tower constantly feed each other**.

### 3. Gameplay Pillars
- **Monster Girl Collection & Raising** – Hatch, name, evolve, mutate, breed. Every girl has visible stats, kinks, and preferences that matter.
- **GuildJobs Management (the naughty core)** – Assign girls to themed rooms and services. Watch client satisfaction and daily earnings skyrocket as you upgrade.
- **Town-Building Around the GuildJobs** – Every new structure is built in the expanding town grid surrounding the central GuildJobs. Buildings visibly improve both raiding and whoring.
- **Mission-Based Tower Expeditions** – No direct control. Choose team, loadout, route, and tactical priorities. Satisfying risk/reward with event logs and lewd outcomes.
- **Systems-Driven Lewdness** – No authored dialogue trees. Lewdness emerges from stats, traits, town upgrades, and player choices (breeding, corruption, specialization).

### 4. Monster Girls (The Collectibles)
- Start with basic **Slime Girls** (squishy, stretchy, high endurance).
- Unlock dozens of species: Succu-Slimes, Lamia, Harpy, Goblin Maid, Mimic Chest Girl, Tentacle Nymph, Dragonette, etc.
- Simple stat model (visible at a glance):
  - **Power** – Combat / obstacle clearing in tower
  - **Charm** – GuildJobs earnings & client satisfaction
  - **Endurance** – Expedition length + shift stamina
  - **Instinct** – Rare loot / lewd event chance
  - **Kink Tags** (Lewd, Submissive, Dominant, Breeding, Corruption, etc.) – directly modify services and synergies
- Progression: Hatching → Training → Mutation (via tower relics or GuildJobs “special nights”) → Breeding (pair two girls or use player “personal service” for bloodline bonuses) → Evolution.

Girls can be injured, stressed, or **delightfully corrupted** (new positive lewd traits). Recovery happens in town spas or by assigning “rest & recreation” in the GuildJobs.

### 5. The GuildJobs (Central Hub & Money Engine)
The GuildJobs is the literal center of the town map. Upgrades unlock:
- New rooms (Vanilla Suite → Tentacle Pit → Breeding Chamber → Public Stage)
- Client types (local adventurers → wealthy nobles → monster diplomats)
- Service tiers (basic handjobs → full harem orgies → custom kink fulfillment)
- Passive bonuses (girls gain Charm while working; Lust Essence generated daily)

GuildJobs income = primary gold source. Higher town support buildings = happier girls = better service = exponential earnings.

### 6. Town Buildings (Support Engine)
All placed on a growing grid around the GuildJobs. Four MVP categories (expandable):

**Habitats** (personalized monster girl housing)  
- Slime Pool, Warm Love Nest, Corruption Garden, Kink Den  
→ Boost specific stats + kink tags + happiness

**Workshops** (gear & consumables)  
- Lust Potion Lab, Silk Rope Forge, Enchanted Toy Workshop  
→ Expedition supplies + GuildJobs performance items

**Recovery & Pleasure Spas**  
- Healing Hot Springs, Aftercare Lounge, Orgasmic Recovery Ward  
→ Faster turnaround, convert injuries into positive corruption

**Research & Specialization**  
- Monster Kink Archive, Tower Seduction Cartography, Breeding Lab  
→ Unlock deeper floors, new client types, rare mutations

Every building visibly changes the town skyline and directly enables new tower depths or sluttier GuildJobs services.

### 7. Tower Expeditions (Mission-Based)
- Choose floor, mission type (Resource Run, Egg Hunt, Corruption Dive, Relic Raid).
- Pick 3–6 girls, assign roles, load gear/potions, set priorities (aggressive, safe, lewd-focused).
- Results delivered via satisfying event log + summary screen with lewd flavor text based on traits.
- Risks: injury, fatigue, or **delicious corruption** (new powerful lewd traits).
- Deeper floors require better town infrastructure and specialized girl teams.

### 8. Resources & Economy
- **Gold** – GuildJobs earnings (main currency for town expansion)
- **Tower Materials** – Used for buildings and gear
- **Eggs / Relics** – New girls and mutations
- **Lust Essence** – Lewd currency for special upgrades, breeding, and corruption events

### 9. Progression & Endgame
- Self-directed goals: Deepest floor reached, Rarest girl collection, Highest daily GuildJobs profit, Most efficient town layout, Ultimate lewd harem.
- Late game: Tower starts “leaking” into the town → optional defense mini-events that reward even lewder content.
- Endless mode after “beating” the tower: perfect your dream brothel town.

### 10. MVP Scope (Minimal Viable Lewd Game)
- 8–10 monster girl species
- 4 tower floors
- Central GuildJobs + 8 town building types
- Basic trait/kink system (10 tags)
- Full core loop implemented with satisfying feedback
- Lewd flavor text + portrait variants (no full animations)

This design stays 100% faithful to the original Slime Tower brainstorm (tight town–tower feedback loop, mission-based expeditions, low art needs, systems-driven progression) while making the **monster brothel the literal and thematic heart** of the experience.

The town literally grows around the GuildJobs, every upgrade makes your girls both better raiders *and* better whores, and the player feels genuine ownership over their growing lewd empire.

Ready to expand into full mechanics, building list, or monster girl roster if you want the next layer.

### 11. MVP Implementation Phases

This implementation plan follows the project documentation:
- **Architecture**: Macroquad is the rendering/input layer only; use explicit game phases and state transitions.
- **Code structure**: Keep data in `assets/`, logic in `engine/`, mutable progression in `state/`, and UI intent-only in `ui/`.
- **UI**: Use `macroquad-toolkit` buttons, panels, progress bars, dark palette, and input helpers instead of custom ad hoc widgets.
- **Scope control**: Build the smallest version of the full loop first, then widen content only after the loop is stable.

#### Phase 1. Project Skeleton and Technical Foundation
**Goal:** Get a runnable game shell that matches the Rust/Macroquad standards before implementing any design systems.

**Deliverables**
- Create the base folder structure from the development guide:
  - `src/data/`
  - `src/engine/`
  - `src/state/`
  - `src/ui/`
  - `assets/`
- Set up `main.rs` with:
  - window config
  - main loop
  - clear background
  - explicit `GamePhase` enum
- Create a top-level `Game` struct responsible for:
  - loaded static data
  - current runtime state
  - update/draw dispatch
  - phase transitions
- Add placeholder JSON files for:
  - constants/config
  - monster species
  - buildings
  - tower floors
  - events/flavor text
- Create `publish.ps1` and confirm `index.html` is valid for Macroquad WebGL.

**Exit criteria**
- Builds on Windows.
- Builds for `wasm32-unknown-unknown`.
- Shows a basic boot flow: `Loading -> MainMenu -> NewGame`.

#### Phase 2. Core Data Model and Save/Load
**Goal:** Define the MVP game state in a data-driven way so feature work does not hardcode balance values.

**Deliverables**
- Data structs in `src/data/` for:
  - `MonsterSpeciesData`
  - `BuildingData`
  - `TowerFloorData`
  - `TraitTagData`
  - `BrothelRoomData`
  - `GameConfig`
- Runtime/save structs in `src/state/` for:
  - `GameState`
  - `PlayerTownState`
  - `MonsterGirlState`
  - `ExpeditionState`
  - `ResourcesState`
  - `SaveData`
- JSON loaders with validation and readable errors.
- Save/load support using JSON with a version field.

**Recommended MVP modeling choices**
- Start with a small fixed set of resource types:
  - Gold
  - Tower Materials
  - Eggs
  - Relics
  - Lust Essence
- Use compact tag lists for kinks/traits rather than deep inheritance or effect graphs.
- Keep monster jobs simple:
  - `Idle`
  - `BrothelShift`
  - `Resting`
  - `OnExpedition`

**Exit criteria**
- A new game can be created, saved, and loaded.
- Static content is loaded from JSON only.
- No critical balance values are hardcoded in gameplay logic.

#### Phase 3. Vertical Slice of the Core Loop
**Goal:** Prove the game is fun in one short repeatable loop before expanding content breadth.

**Deliverables**
- Implement one starter species:
  - Slime Girl
- Implement one guild room:
  - Vanilla Suite
- Implement one support building:
  - Slime Pool
- Implement one expedition floor:
  - Floor 1 resource run
- Implement one day resolution cycle:
  - assign girls
  - resolve brothel income
  - resolve expedition result
  - apply fatigue/injury/trait outcomes
  - award resources
- Add a summary/results screen for daily outcomes.

**Core systems to finish here**
- Assignment flow
- Resource gain/spend flow
- Simple recovery flow
- Simple event log/flavor text generation

**Exit criteria**
- Player can hatch a starter girl, assign her, run a day, gain rewards, and spend those rewards on one meaningful upgrade.
- The loop is understandable without placeholder debug commands.

#### Phase 4. GuildJobs Management MVP
**Goal:** Turn the brothel into the main money engine with enough surface area to support planning and specialization.

**Deliverables**
- GuildJobs screen with:
  - room list
  - available workers
  - expected income preview
  - girl status display
- Basic service resolution using:
  - Charm
  - Endurance
  - relevant trait tags
- Add 3 to 4 room/service types total for MVP.
- Add client tier progression in simple form:
  - Local Adventurers
  - Wealthy Nobles
  - Monster Diplomats
- Generate:
  - gold income
  - lust essence
  - happiness/stress changes

**Design constraints**
- UI returns actions only; all state mutation happens in main game/state orchestration.
- Keep formulas transparent and easy to rebalance from JSON.

**Exit criteria**
- GuildJobs choices create visible differences in earnings and girl condition.
- There is a clear reason to optimize workers rather than assign randomly.

#### Phase 5. Tower Expedition MVP
**Goal:** Make expeditions the second half of the loop, focused on team assembly and outcome resolution rather than direct control.

**Deliverables**
- Expedition planning screen with:
  - floor selection
  - mission type selection
  - team selection
  - priority/order selection
- Implement MVP mission types:
  - Resource Run
  - Egg Hunt
  - Relic Raid
  - Corruption Dive
- Resolve expeditions through stat checks and event tables using:
  - Power
  - Endurance
  - Instinct
  - trait tags
- Add risks:
  - injury
  - fatigue
  - positive corruption trait gain

**Content target for this phase**
- Expand to all 4 MVP floors.
- Add a small reusable event pool per floor/mission type.

**Exit criteria**
- Expeditions reliably return differentiated rewards and setbacks.
- Team composition and priorities matter enough to change outcomes.

#### Phase 6. Town Building and Progression Web
**Goal:** Connect brothel performance and expedition depth through visible town expansion.

**Deliverables**
- Central town layout screen with the GuildJobs as the anchor.
- Implement the 8 MVP building types across four categories:
  - Habitats
  - Workshops
  - Recovery & Pleasure Spas
  - Research & Specialization
- Buildings provide explicit unlocks or modifiers to:
  - guild rooms/services
  - expedition access
  - mutation/breeding options
  - recovery speed
- Add prerequisite logic for deeper floors and advanced services.

**Recommended rule**
- Every building must improve either:
  - expedition capability
  - brothel output
  - or both

If a building does neither, it does not belong in the MVP.

**Exit criteria**
- Town growth visibly changes player options.
- The player can understand why each constructed building matters to the loop.

#### Phase 7. Monster Variety, Traits, and Content Pass
**Goal:** Expand from the vertical slice into the promised MVP content count without changing the core loop structure.

**Deliverables**
- Increase roster to 8 to 10 species.
- Implement approximately 10 trait/kink tags.
- Add portrait/icon support for species and status traits.
- Add mutation/evolution hooks in simple form.
- Add more flavor text variants for:
  - brothel shifts
  - expedition outcomes
  - corruption gains

**Scope protection**
- No complex breeding simulation yet.
- No authored dialogue trees.
- No animation-heavy content requirements.

**Exit criteria**
- Different species and tags support noticeably different build strategies.
- Content variety increases replayability without adding system bloat.

#### Phase 8. UX, Balance, and MVP Hardening
**Goal:** Make the game reliable, legible, and ready for external playtesting.

**Deliverables**
- Add tooltips and clear stat explanations.
- Improve onboarding:
  - simple first-run hints
  - readable resource explanations
  - obvious assignment flow
- Balance income, recovery, and floor gating.
- Add missing guardrails:
  - invalid assignment prevention
  - save migration/version handling
  - missing data fallback/logging
- Run:
  - `cargo fmt`
  - `cargo clippy`
  - core unit tests for calculations, transitions, and loaders

**Exit criteria**
- New players can understand the loop without extra documentation.
- The game runs cleanly on both Windows and Web targets.
- MVP is stable enough for iteration based on feedback instead of structural rework.

### 12. Recommended Initial Screen/Phase List

Use explicit phases from the start so scope stays controlled:
- `Loading`
- `MainMenu`
- `TownOverview`
- `BrothelManagement`
- `ExpeditionPlanning`
- `DayResults`
- `SaveLoadModal`

This is enough for the MVP. Do not add extra screens until one of these becomes overloaded.

### 13. Recommended First Milestone

If development starts immediately, the first milestone should be:
- one species
- one guild room
- one building
- one tower floor
- one complete day cycle
- save/load working

That milestone is the highest-value proof that the design works. Everything else should expand from it rather than being built in parallel.
