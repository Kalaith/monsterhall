# Monsterhall Phases 5-10 Implementation Notes

This pass completes the remaining GDD transition as compatibility-preserving hooks rather than a save-breaking rebuild.

## Phase 5: Town Jobs and Guild Services

- Guild rooms now carry explicit town-job metadata: `job_kind`, `base_materials_yield`, `reputation_yield`, `preparation_quality_bonus`, and `recovery_bonus`.
- Guild job previews now show gold, materials, residue, preparation quality, reputation, recovery support, and work history.
- Day resolution now applies material output, companion reputation gain, and recovery support from town jobs.

## Phase 6: Contract Desk Upgrade

- Contracts now carry `category`, `patron_tier_id`, `preparation_quality_required`, and `preparation_quality_bonus`.
- Active contract state stores those fields when contracts are generated or chained.
- Contract availability respects patron tier unlocks.
- Partial success now checks town preparation quality, which is generated by guild jobs, accepted contracts, and town projects.

## Phase 7: Expedition Risk and Tower Identity

- Expedition priorities now include `RecoveryFocused` alongside Safe, Balanced, Aggressive, and Curiosity.
- Recovery priority reduces injury pressure and sacrifices reward aggression.
- Existing floor hazard tags, mission risk modifiers, species/role affinity, and preparation outputs now form the transition surface for deeper tower-risk work.

## Phase 8: Hatchery and Recruitment

- Hatchery copy and recruitment framing now treats eggs as guild roster growth rather than the old chamber framing.
- Contract rewards and expedition rewards remain the first recruitment pressure valves through eggs, residue, relics, and reputation.
- Egg preparation focus remains the extension point for future utility/resilience/social hatchery choices.

## Phase 9: Economy and Pressure

- Player-facing upkeep language now reads as operating costs: wages, supplies, and repairs.
- Internal save/config field names remain stable for compatibility.
- Town jobs, contracts, expeditions, debt, and upkeep now each have distinct reward or pressure hooks.

## Phase 10: Presentation and Art Direction

- Backdrop metadata now points to guild jobs, hatchery/nursery care, tower route planning, hazard prep, and cozy logistics plus danger.
- UI labels now emphasize contracts, town jobs, preparation quality, recovery, patron tiers, and arcane residue.

## Compatibility Boundary

- Legacy asset filenames such as `guest_requests.json`, `guild_rooms.json`, and `client_tiers.json` are retained.
- Legacy serde aliases remain for old saves and older data keys.
- Internal resource field `arcane_residue` remains serialized as-is but is presented as arcane residue.
